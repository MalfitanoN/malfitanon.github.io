1. Battlestar Galactica

This App Academy Projects Goal was to create a multiplayer game. The game I chose to do was a simple shooter where if you hit your enemy you get a point. The game has very simple controls for Player 1 (Red) is the arrow keys to move and M to shoot for Player 2 (Green) the controls are WASD and Q to shoot. The game is called Battle Star Galactica because you battle in space and they skins for the players are from Galaga. We used socket.io and p5.js for this project sockets allows you to host your project on a server and make it multiplayer and p5 is what allows you to draw and make the game. Overall what i like about this project was the freedom we had to create whatever type of game we wanted.
